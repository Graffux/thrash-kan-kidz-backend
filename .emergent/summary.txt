<original_problem_statement>
The user wants to build a mobile card collecting app for their Thrash Kan Kidz cards.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Core Gameplay**: Users log in, receive coins, and can purchase cards. A collection view shows all cards, indicating which are owned and which are missing.
- **Card Tiers & Unlocks**:
    - **Common Cards**: Purchasable from the shop.
    - **Rare Cards**: Two cards (Martin Van Druid, Tardy Donald) that become purchasable for 75 coins after collecting 10 and 20 total cards, respectively.
    - **Epic Cards**: Two cards (Tom Angeltipper, Tom Angelflipper) that become purchasable for 100 coins after achieving 7-day and 14-day consecutive login streaks.
    - **Milestone Cards**: Users receive one free, random, unowned common card every time their collection size grows by 5 cards.
    - **Coming Soon Cards**: A set of cards that are visible in the shop but marked as unavailable for purchase.
- **Card Interaction**: Cards in the collection should flip over to show the back when swiped. Coming Soon cards should not be flippable.
- **UI/UX**:
    - Custom logo and background on the login screen and all other screens.
    - A Rare Card and Epic Card section in the shop to show progress.
    - A celebration pop-up (Modal) when a Rare, Epic, or Milestone card is unlocked/awarded.
    - A user profile/dashboard screen for stats and logging out.
    - Icons for tab navigation.
- **App Store Preparation**: The app should be configured with an icon, splash screen, and necessary metadata for submission to the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
</original_problem_statement>

**what currently exists?**
A full-stack Expo (React Native) and FastAPI application with a MongoDB database. The app features a tab-based navigation system with screens for Home (Login), Collection, Shop, Goals, Trade, and a new Profile screen.

The backend manages users, cards, and all game logic, including a robust seeding mechanism that handles creating and updating cards on startup. Three tiers of cards (Common, Rare, Epic) are implemented with different unlock mechanics (collection size, login streaks). A milestone system awards free common cards.

The frontend displays cards in a Shop and a Collection view. It features a swipe-to-flip card component, celebration modals for unlocks, and progress indicators for Rare and Epic cards.
